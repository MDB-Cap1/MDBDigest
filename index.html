The Oral Small-Molecule GLP-1 Race: Beyond Orforglipron

Wegovy and Zepbound built a $71 billion market by treating less than 5% of eligible patients. The other 95% remain untreated — not because the drugs don't work, but because needles, cold chains, and $1,000-per-month pricing don't scale. Fifty oral small-molecule programs are now racing to close that gap. PatentVest reviewed over 1,200 patent documents across 26 programs to find out which will become twenty-year franchises and which become commodities.
